# ✈️ Airline Business Intelligence & Revenue Analytics System

## 📌 Project Overview

The Airline Business Intelligence & Revenue Analytics System is an end-to-end Data Analytics project developed to analyze airline business performance, identify revenue drivers, evaluate pricing strategies, and generate actionable business insights.

This project demonstrates the practical application of Python, SQL, and Power BI to solve real-world business problems in the aviation industry through data-driven decision making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze airline revenue performance.
* Identify the most profitable routes.
* Understand customer booking behavior.
* Evaluate airline pricing strategies.
* Measure airline market share.
* Generate business intelligence insights.
* Support strategic business decision-making.

## 📌 Key Business Insights

* Premium airlines generate the highest revenue.
* Business class contributes significantly to overall revenue.
* Metropolitan routes demonstrate the highest profitability.
* Advance booking generally results in lower ticket prices.
* Airline market share is concentrated among a few major carriers.
* Pricing strategies have a direct impact on revenue optimization.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

* Focus operations on high-performing routes.
* Optimize dynamic pricing strategies.
* Expand premium-class offerings.
* Encourage advance bookings through promotional pricing.
* Improve revenue management systems.
* Expand operations in high-demand markets.
